About Us

Howrah Vigyan Chetna Samannaya (HVCS), established in 1984 by passionate science activists, educators, and nature enthusiasts, stands as a registered science club (Regd. No: S90891 of 1998-99). This esteemed club holds the distinguished VIPNET Membership of VIGYAN PRASAR under the Department of Science & Technology, Government of India (Unique Club Authorization Number: VP-WB0055). HVCS passionately promotes science through environmental conservation programs, hands-on experiments, anti-superstition programs, night sky observation, amateur astronomy, nature study camps, nature treks, diverse science workshops, and by enhancing elementary-level scientific knowledge and social understanding among students. HVCS operates as the District Coordinator of Howrah in National Children's Science Congress. Its health service unit, Swasthya CHETNA, actively serves the community through awareness programs and medical camps.
